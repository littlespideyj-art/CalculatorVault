Privacy Policy – Calculator Hide

Calculator Hide is a privacy vault app designed to help users store and protect personal photos, videos, and files securely on their device.

Information We Collect:

* The app may access device storage to allow users to select and hide photos, videos, and files.
* The app may use the camera if the user chooses to capture media.
* The app uses internet access to display ads via third-party services (AdMob).

Data Usage:

* All files selected by the user remain stored locally on the device.
* We do not collect or store personal data on external servers.
* We do not sell or share personal user data.

Third-Party Services:

* This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymized data such as device information to improve ad experience.

User Control:

* Users can remove files from the vault at any time.
* Users can uninstall the app to remove all stored data.

Security:

* We aim to protect user data locally within the app environment.
